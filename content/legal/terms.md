## 7. Pre-orders, shipping timing, and your FTC cancellation rights (16 CFR Part 435)

Our pre-orders are governed by the **FTC Mail, Internet, or Telephone Order Merchandise
Rule, 16 CFR Part 435**. In plain terms:

- **We ship within the time we promise.** When you place a properly completed order, we
  will ship within the time stated on the product/checkout page. **If we state no time,
  we will ship within 30 days** of your properly completed order (the "30-day rule").
  For made-to-order Drop 1 items we will **clearly state the expected ship window**
  (e.g., "ships on or about August 2026") at checkout, and that stated window governs.
- **If we can't ship on time, we'll tell you and give you a choice.** If we learn we
  cannot ship within the stated time (or 30 days if none stated), we will promptly send
  you a notice with a revised shipping date and **the option to (a) consent to the
  delay or (b) cancel your order for a full and prompt refund**.
- **Silence after a definite revised date.** If our notice offers a **definite** revised
  shipping date 30 days or less later and you do not respond, we may treat that as your
  consent to the new date, as the Rule permits. For longer or indefinite delays we will
  **not** ship unless you **affirmatively** consent; otherwise we cancel and refund.
- **Prompt refunds.** When you are owed a refund under this section, we will refund by a
  method at least as fast and reliable as first-class mail. For card payments via
  Stripe, we will issue the refund to your card promptly — generally **within one
  billing cycle** — and for any non-card method **within seven (7) working days** of
  your refund right vesting, consistent with 16 CFR 435.2.

These cancellation and refund rights are **in addition to** the voluntary
