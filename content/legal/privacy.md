## 3. How we use personal data (purposes)

- **Provide the Services:** create/maintain your account and Founding Member status,
  process pre-orders and payments, manage store credit, ship products, handle returns.
- **Customer support and transactional messages:** order confirmations, shipping/delay
  notices (including FTC option notices), refund notices.

  notices, newsletters — always with an unsubscribe.
- **Referral and promotions:** validate referrals and apply credits; detect abuse.
- **Security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and analytics** to operate and
  improve the Services.

## 4. Legal bases (for EU/UK visitors — GDPR/UK GDPR)

Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies, we rely on: **performance of a contract** (orders, account,
credit), **consent** (marketing emails; withdrawable anytime), **legitimate interests**
(security, fraud prevention, service improvement), and **legal obligation** (tax,
recordkeeping). See § 13.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards (encryption in

## 10. Children's privacy

The Services are for adults **18+**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from

## 11. Your U.S. privacy rights

### 11.1 Rights available to U.S. residents under enacted state laws

Depending on your state, you may have some or all of these rights:

- **Access / Know** the personal data we hold about you;
- **Correct** inaccuracies;
- **Delete** your personal data (subject to legal exceptions);
- **Portability** — get a copy in a portable format;
- **Opt out** of (i) **targeted advertising**, (ii) **sale** of personal data, and
  (iii) certain **profiling** with significant effects;
- **Limit use of sensitive data** (where applicable);
- **Non-discrimination** for exercising rights; and
- **Appeal** a denied request (in states that require an appeal process).

**Our baseline:** We do **not sell** your personal data and do **not** conduct targeted
advertising or significant profiling in the no-pixel configuration. If that changes, we
will provide a **"Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information"** link and honor
opt-out-preference signals (e.g., Global Privacy Control) as required.

### 11.2 How to exercise your rights

Email **support@chariotarchive.com** (subject: "Privacy Request") or use any
[in-app privacy request form — FOUNDER DECISION to add]. We will verify your identity
(usually via your account email) and respond within the time your state requires
(generally **45 days**, extendable once). You may use an **authorized agent** where the
law allows.

### 11.3 California notice at collection (CCPA/CPRA)

At or before collection, California residents are told: the **categories** of personal
data we collect (see § 2), the **purposes** (see § 3), that we **do not sell or share**
personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising in our baseline configuration, and
our **retention** approach (see § 8). California residents also have the right to **limit
use of sensitive personal information** (we do not use sensitive PI for inferring
characteristics) and to **non-discrimination**.
