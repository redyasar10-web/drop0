A Letter from the Founders · Chariot · May 2026

Dear Founding Crew,

1NRI has been making clothes out of a studio in Berekuso since 2018. Nana Kwadwo Osei Nyarko runs it out of Ashesi University, where he was a student.

Jireh works out of East Legon, in Accra. The Battle Angel series is theirs. We brought both brands to the United States this year because no one was going to do it for them.

Chariot is not a marketplace. We are not a wholesaler. We are the bridge — Accra to Austin, through Aquantuo, under AGOA, paying the brands what they actually charge.

The Founding Crew is the first fifty people who put their hand up before any of this proves out. You will be on the hangtag of every Chariot piece, in the order you joined. The numbers stay private. The trust does not.

When Drop 1 opens in July, you go first. Twenty-four hours before anyone else. Every drop after that, same thing. Permanently.

That's the entire pitch.

Red, on behalf of the Chariot team
Wooster, Ohio
